# Default divorce in NYC

When a defendant is properly served with divorce papers and does not respond within the required time, the plaintiff may proceed toward a default judgment. This is a distinct workflow from uncontested divorce where both parties participate.

CourtFlow collects service date, grounds, and county information required for the default matrimonial packet.

## Key facts the intake will ask

- Service date and method
- Whether minor children are involved
- County of filing

Always confirm current NYSCEF and local part requirements for your county before filing default papers.
