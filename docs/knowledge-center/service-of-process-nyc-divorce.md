# Service of process in NYC divorce

In a matrimonial action, the plaintiff typically cannot personally serve the defendant. Service is usually completed by another adult who is not a party to the case.

CourtFlow provides informational guidance only. Verify current court rules for your county.

## What to track

- Date of service
- Who completed service
- Affidavit of Service for your records
