# Orders of protection in NYC

An order of protection (OP) is a court order that limits contact to help keep someone safe. In NYC, many OP matters begin in Family Court, though Supreme Court matrimonial cases may involve overlapping relief.

CourtFlow routes OP requests to the appropriate workflow and forms based on your facts — it does not evaluate safety risk or legal strategy.

## Before you start

- Know which NYC county applies
- Be prepared to describe the relationship and what relief you seek
- Review local court safety resources if you are in immediate danger — call 911 for emergencies
