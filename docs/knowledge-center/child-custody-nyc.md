# Child custody in NYC Family Court

Custody cases in NYC Family Court determine legal and physical custody arrangements for children. The court focuses on the best interests of the child.

Use CourtFlow intake to confirm Family Court routing, collect required facts, and obtain the correct blank forms for your county.

## Common required information

- County of filing
- Number of children and basic identifying details
- Parent/guardian contact information

## Important note

Custody may overlap with an active Supreme Court divorce. CourtFlow explains overlap scenarios but does not choose strategy for you.
