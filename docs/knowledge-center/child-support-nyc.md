# Child support in NYC Family Court

Child support matters in NYC are typically handled in Family Court. CourtFlow helps identify the child support workflow, required forms, and procedural next steps after intake.

## What CourtFlow collects

- Filing county
- Number of children
- Basic party information needed for commencement forms

Support amounts and enforcement strategy are legal determinations outside CourtFlow's scope.
