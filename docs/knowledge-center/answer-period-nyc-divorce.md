# Answer period in NYC divorce

After service, a defendant may file an answer, default, or take other responsive steps depending on the circumstances.

CourtFlow uses deterministic deadline rules for informational estimates. These are not legal advice and may not reflect every case.

## What CourtFlow tracks

- Service date you confirm
- Informational answer deadline estimate
- Whether your spouse answered or did not respond in time
