# Property and debt in NYC divorce

Divorce matters often involve bank accounts, real estate, vehicles, retirement accounts, credit card debt, and business interests.

CourtFlow collects facts to help identify forms and procedural steps. It does not value assets or recommend division strategy.

## Intake topics

- Marital assets and debts
- Whether property issues are resolved (uncontested path)
- Income information for support-related forms
